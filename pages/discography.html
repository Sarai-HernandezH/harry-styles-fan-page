Random facts

Did you know that Harry used to work at a bakery when he was 16 years old before his auditiong for the X-factor?

His mother signed him up for the show without Harry's knowledge and just told him a couple of weeks before of the audtions.

His music is inspired by the artists he grew up listening to, such as Pink Floyd, the Rolling Stones, the Beatles and Fleetwood Mac, as well as the songwriting of Harry Nilsson.

Harry has recently broken a few records: with One direction by being the first UK group to debut at number one in the USA with a debut album and winning the most Teen Choice Awards by a music group with 28. On his solo career: has been confirmed as the most streamed track on Spotify (2022),beat Mark Ronson and Sir Elton John - who held the joint record for a UK artist holding the top of the Billborad charts. Mark Ronson's Uptown Funk and Sir Elton John's Candle In The Wind both spent 14 weeks at number one.
